Project: AquaSense® IoT Water Monitoring Application.
A full stack React-Redux web application.

This application was built to monitor water temperature and water levels in my koi pond.

This was a very fun challenge! I wired and programmed a wifi IoT device connected to a water temperature sensor and a liquid level sensor, then installed them in the pond. The water level sensor was installed behind the skimmer filter in the pump chamber. This sensor will alert me of low water, I can clear debris from the filter before any damage to the pump occurs. The data is being logged every 5 seconds to a PostgreSQL database. The application pulls that data every minute. Graphs display the averages over a 24 hour period, and a data table gives me up-to-the minute readings.
