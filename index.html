Diving into new technologies one project at a time.

There is not much I enjoy more than tackling a project which requires me to get out of my box.

In the process of researching, building, and writing, my IoT water monitoring application, I discover I had deep and profound desire to learn as much as I can about of the Internet of Things. The excitement I feel for this technology is on par with events in my past that have shaped my life. Standing on the starting grid my first day on a pro motorcyle race team! The first time my old band played a sold out show, that we headlined! My first helicopter ride, with a pilot that showed me all of the "tricks" he could do, yikes! The vastness of possibities with the Internet of Things reminds me of what pioneers must have felt when they first looked out over the Grand Canyon, and I want to stake my claim!
