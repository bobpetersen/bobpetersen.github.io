Project: AquaSense®, IoT Water Monitoring Application.
A full stack React-Redux web application.

This application was built to monitor water temperature and levels in a koi pond.

This was a very fun project! I wired and and programmed a wifi IoT device connected to a water temperature sensor and a liquid level sensor. I then installed them in the pond. The water level sensor was placed behind the skimmer filter in the pump chamber. The level sensor will alert the user of low water allowing the them ample time to clear debris before any damage to the pumps occurs. The data is being logged every 5 seconds to a PostgreSQL database. The application pulls that data every minute. I have graphs to show the averages over a 24 hour period and a table that give users an up to the minute readings.
